How It Works

  1. Face Detection: Uses Haar Cascade classifiers to detect faces in images or video frames
  2. Image Preprocessing: Resizes and normalizes detected face regions for model input
  3. Classification: Deep learning model analyzes the face region to determine mask presence
  4. Results Display: Shows prediction with confidence score and visual indicators
